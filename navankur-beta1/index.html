Our Agri Services

E-marketplace

Our platform serves as a marketplace for the agriculture sector, connecting farmers, FPOs, wholesalers, retailers, and traders to facilitate the buying and selling of agricultural products in a centralized location. Additionally, we provide updates on agriculture news and new technology advancements, as well as information on agriculture loan schemes and agriculture land purchase loans to assist students interested in agriculture projects.

E-Auction

We established a system for bidding and transactions in Agri-Commodity procurement, ensuring transparency at all stages and making it easier for traders to conduct business. This mechanism can benefit various agricultural services, such as agricultural seed procurement and agricultural science research, as well as agricultural financing options like agricultural loans, agricultural land loans, and agricultural insurance.

Advisory

We provide consultancy services through our Agri-Experts and empower the farmers by providing real-time information. The farmers’ queries are stored in a database along with their relevant solutions.

Our Presence

Our mission at Dehaat Company is to expand our presence across multiple states in India, while also aiming to penetrate the global market. We strive for sustainable agricultural development and growth, specifically in crop production. Our goal is to empower farmers by providing them with the tools and knowledge necessary to become self-reliant entrepreneurs. To support this mission, we promote the benefits of organic farming and utilize advanced technologies such as agriculture spray drones to improve crop yields and reduce costs.
