Selection of honeybee according to Subhash Chand

There are many bee species available, but Subhash Chand chose Apis Mellifera as this species is easy to manage and at the same time, can produce high yield. The enzyme such as invertase, diastase, glucose, and oxidase, present in these bees when getting in contact with the nectar, can make the honey they produce nutritious and can have many health benefits.
