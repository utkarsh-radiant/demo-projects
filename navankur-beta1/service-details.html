Soil Testing

In modern agriculture practices, soil testing is essential to manage the application of fertilizer as well as crop production, especially in the non-farming sector. At Navankur, we offer tailor-made results through soil-testing kits to our suppliers to perform quality checks of the soil for various types of farming, including organic farms. Testing of soil is performed with the latest technology to improve the quality of the ground, as well as to identify deficiencies presented in the soil as macro as well as micronutrients. Under parameters like pH level characteristics, you may further enhance the fertility level of the ground. Our soil testing services ensure you always grow healthy crops while preventing the environment from becoming contaminated. Soil testing is an essential procedure for several reasons, including optimizing crop production as well as clarifying the absence of any nutritional balance in the soil, which is crucial for the success of any farming operation.

  • Helps to understand the growth potential of the soil
  • Finds out the nutrition deficiency
  • Provides cost-effective method
